Downloads

Lossless Offline Caching

Save high-quality MP3s directly to your local internal storage directory for offline playback. VibeStream features direct stream query redirects: if a primary stream fails, fallbacks are loaded immediately to ensure your download finishes.

  • High-quality audio extraction
  • Smart piped fallback resolvers
  • Deletes cached files completely on logout
Ad-Free

100% Ad-Block Streaming

Client-side background WebView overrides intercept video and audio advertisements, fast-forwarding ads at 16x speed and muting them automatically. Enjoy pure, uninterrupted listening without subscriptions.

  • 16x speed fast-forwarding & muting
  • Background visibility state patches
  • Seamless background WebView playback
Autoplay

Smart Autoplay Queue

Never hear silence. VibeStream analyzes your currently playing track, searches live public Invidious instance registries, and automatically populates the "Up Next" queue before the active track concludes.

  • Automatic recommendation fetches
  • Live public instance fallbacks
  • Flawless crossfade transition flow
Cloud Sync

Continuous Firebase Backup

Your library is saved online in real-time. Any modification to custom playlists, favorites lists, settings, or search history is synchronized to your Firebase Realtime Database path `/users/$uid`.

  • Syncs on every modification
  • Bypasses onboarding screens on returning logins
  • Restores favorites, playlists, and settings instantly
Customization

Responsive Waveforms

Cycle between three customized, reactive visualizers built in Jetpack Compose to fit your style: Classic Waveform, Linear Spectrum, or Circle Blast. Configure sleep timers to fade audio as you drift off.

  • 3 reactive visualizer styles
  • Gradual audio fade sleep timers
  • Fluid glassmorphic controls
